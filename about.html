What is Makrall?

Makrall began as a simple group chat on Instagram. Over time, sections of conversation were collected as screenshots, many of which becoming slightly humerous without any context. From there, the idea was born to illustrate these snippets as comics with an art style similar to that of xkcd.com. The Makrall website was then created for a school project to showcase the comics, as well other projects done by the group.

Since then, the origanal cast of Makrall has migrated to TofuOreobellum, (a Discord group chat) absorbing a few new members in the process. Fortunately, there still remains a backlog of screenshots from Instagram, which will be converted into comic form sometime in the near future.
